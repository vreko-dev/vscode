## Intelligence Platform

Vreko is an **Intelligence Platform**, not just a backup tool.

### What Triggers Intelligence?
- Critical files modified (.env, auth, config)
- High-risk change patterns detected
- Multiple files changed rapidly

### The Progression
- **Day 1**: Detection  -  See what AI touches
- **Day 30**: Patterns  -  Know what causes issues
- **Day 90**: Predictions  -  Catch problems before they ship

Every session makes your codebase smarter.
